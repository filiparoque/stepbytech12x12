Where we started

12x12 started as an exhibtion/ event, organized by a group of students from the Faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Lisbon. The premises were simple: on the same walls, 12x12 cm artworks are exhibited, inside or attached to a cd case, with no captions or information on the artwork or artist. All the artworks are sold at the same price.
