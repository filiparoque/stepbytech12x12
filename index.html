How we started

12x12 started as a small exhibtion, in a studio, organized by a group of students from the Faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Lisbon. The premises were simple: on the same walls, 12x12 cm artworks are exhibited, inside or attached to a cd case, with no captions or information on the artwork or artist. All the artworks displayed are available at the same price.

Where we are now

Being held every year, we are now at our 13th Edition. Over the years we have welcomed more and more artists, changed to bigger locations, and other editions were made with larger works, always in a square format. Come and join us with your paintings, drawings, printmakings, or just come and pay us a visit in our 2023 Edition
